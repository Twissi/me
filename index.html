I built my first website in 1999. It was stuffed with tables and background-images, but I loved it. I created pages for friends or random people from the internet just for fun. After finishing school I began to study Computer Science in Dresden. Everything I learned there was totally different than creating a static website.
For my diploma thesis I moved to Munich to work with researcher from Fraunhofer ESK on ELEPHANT, a tool to generate mobile apps. I got fascinated in the mobile field and wanted to learn from the experts. So I started to work for CELLULAR, an agency known for their mobile solutions. After 3 years of building great mobile websites it is now time for new challenges.
