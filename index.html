I built my first website in 1999. It was stuffed with tables, background-images and spacer.gifs. But I fell in love. I created pages for friends or random people from the internet just for fun. After finishing school I began to study Computer Science in Dresden to learn more about computer technology. Everything I learned there was totally different from creating a static website.
For my diploma thesis I moved to Munich to work as a researcher at Fraunhofer ESK on a project called ELEPHANT. The aim was to create a tool that helps people to generate context sensitive mobile apps. While writing my thesis I got fascinated with the mobile field. Because I wanted to learn from the experts in mobile development, I applied as a developer at CELLULAR, an agency known for their mobile solutions.

After 3 years working for CELLULAR on many different projects and building great mobile websites I wanted to try something new. I was looking for an opportunity to combine working for clients on new challenging projects and also having more time to spend on side projects and things that are important to me.

Today I am working as a contracter for different companies and projects. I love to be involved in the startup and coworking scene in Vienna and I like to support female developers and entrepreneurs.
